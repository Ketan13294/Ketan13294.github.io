Kapi Ketan Mehta

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Virginia Tech, where my reserach is on control and planning for autonomous systems. My PhD advisor is Kaveh Akbari Hamed.

From 2023 to 2024, I worked at Johns Hopkins University, as part of Dynamical Systems and Control Laboratory under the guidance of Dr. Louis Whitcomb.

I have an MS in Robotics from Johns Hopkins University, where I was a research assistant for Dr. Louis Whitcomb and Dr. Simon Leonard. I was also a teaching assistant for Dr. Jin Seob Kim (EN.530.646 Robot Devices, Kinematics, Dynamics, and Control and EN.530.624 Dynamics of Robots and Spacecrafts), Dr. Simon Leonard (EN.530.707 Robot System Programming), and Dr. Mathias Unberath(EN.601.482 Machine Learning: Deep Learning). I have a BS in ME from BITS Pilani.
